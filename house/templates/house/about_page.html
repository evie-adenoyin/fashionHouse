About us

Fashion House is a place for fashion designers to showcase their designs , it is a place for people to find the right fashion designers to design their clothes for them , so maybe you are a work from home fashion designer who has a machine but doesn't have a shop , Fashion house is your shop,it is a place where fashion designers/tailors are connected to people who are intrested in finding the right fashion designer for their products, it is also a place where investors meet good fashion designers to partner with them, hire them or run contracts with them. Fashion house is a place where we do everything about designing clothes , we are so intrested in the run way that we help fashion designers showcase their talents, design, skills and potentials so people who are looking for a fashion designer can get the best of them.

In fashion House , people who are looking for a good fashion designer would connect with the tailors and fashion designers here to make and deliver their clothes to them at home without any stress it is a place where your design meets the market. Fashion House is a free website for everyone to post their designs and attract potential clients.
